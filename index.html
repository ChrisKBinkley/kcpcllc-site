About KCPC

Built on Experience.
Driven by Craft.

Kansas City Premier Construction is a full-service general contractor specializing in the commercial real estate market. We founded this company on a simple belief: commercial construction should be straightforward, professionally managed, and delivered with integrity.

We've spent years working alongside property owners, real estate developers, and business tenants across the Kansas City metro — learning what they need, what frustrates them, and how a good contractor can make all the difference. KCPC was built to be that contractor: responsive, skilled, and accountable from day one to project close.

We're proud to be a local company serving a city we know well. The Kansas City commercial real estate market has unique rhythms, codes, and relationships — and we work within that world every day.
